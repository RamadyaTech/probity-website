Data Processing Agreement

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the agreement between ProbityGRC ("Processor," "we," "us") and the subscribing entity ("Controller," "Customer," "you") for the ProbityGRC services. This DPA governs how we process personal data on your behalf in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR.

1. Scope of Processing

We process personal data only as necessary to provide the ProbityGRC platform, in accordance with your documented instructions. This includes:

  • Data subjects: Your employees, contractors, and authorized platform users
  • Data types: Names, email addresses, job titles, roles, IP addresses, login credentials, and activity logs
  • Purpose: User authentication, access control, audit trails, and compliance reporting
  • Duration: For the term of your service agreement

2. Our Obligations

As Processor, we will:

  • Process personal data only on your documented instructions
  • Ensure authorized personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  • Assist you with data subject rights requests
  • Delete or return all personal data upon termination, at your choice
  • Make information available for compliance audits upon reasonable notice

3. Sub-processors

We may engage third-party sub-processors to help deliver the platform. We maintain a current list on our sub-processors page. We will notify you at least 30 days before engaging a new sub-processor, and you may object within 15 days.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect personal data, including encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, network security, monitoring, regular backups, and vulnerability management. See our Security Practices page for details.

5. Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware, provide relevant details, and cooperate in investigation and remediation.

6. Data Transfers

We will not transfer personal data outside the EEA without appropriate safeguards in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or an adequacy decision.

7. Data Retention

Upon termination, you may export your data for 30 days. After that, we will delete all personal data within 60 days unless retention is required by law.

8. Term

This DPA is effective for the duration of your service agreement. Obligations regarding data deletion, confidentiality, and audit rights survive termination.

9. Contact

For data protection inquiries, contact us at dpo@probitygrc.com.
