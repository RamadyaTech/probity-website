Security Practices

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Our Commitment

Security is foundational to ProbityGRC. We build our platform with security best practices at every layer and continuously invest in protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your data.

Infrastructure

ProbityGRC is hosted on enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure. Our environments use network isolation, firewalls with least-privilege rules, DDoS mitigation, and infrastructure-as-code for consistency and auditability.

Data Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols. Encryption keys are managed through dedicated key management services and stored separately from encrypted data.

Access Controls

The platform provides role-based access control (RBAC) with granular permissions. Multi-factor authentication is available for all accounts. Internal access to production systems follows the principle of least privilege.

Application Security

Our development practices include mandatory code reviews, automated security scanning in our CI/CD pipeline, dependency monitoring, and regular third-party penetration testing.

Tenant Isolation

ProbityGRC is a multi-tenant application with strict data isolation. Each customer's data is logically separated at the database level, and all queries are scoped to the authenticated tenant.

Monitoring & Incident Response

We maintain comprehensive audit logging, intrusion detection, and automated alerting for security events. In the event of a confirmed data breach, we will notify affected customers within 72 hours in compliance with applicable regulations.

Business Continuity

We perform regular encrypted backups stored in geographically separate locations. Critical components are deployed with redundancy, and disaster recovery procedures are tested periodically.

Compliance

Our security program aligns with ISO 27001, SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria, GDPR, and India's DPDP Act. We are continuously expanding our compliance posture — contact us for the latest information.

Vulnerability Disclosure

We welcome responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities. Please report any findings to security@probitygrc.com. We will acknowledge receipt within 2 business days and will not pursue action against good-faith researchers.
