About Probity

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Who We Are

ProbityGRC is a technology company focused on simplifying governance, risk, and compliance for modern organizations. We combine deep domain expertise in regulatory compliance with modern software engineering to deliver a platform that teams actually want to use.

Our Mission

Enterprise-grade GRC has traditionally been reserved for large corporations with dedicated compliance departments and six-figure software budgets. We believe that growing organizations deserve the same level of structure and visibility without the complexity or cost. Our mission is to make governance, risk, and compliance accessible, practical, and affordable for every organization that takes compliance seriously.

Our Vision

We envision a world where compliance is a growth enabler, not a bottleneck. Probity is designed to help organizations build trust with customers, partners, and regulators by embedding compliance into their daily workflows rather than treating it as a periodic checkbox exercise.

Our Team

The team behind Probity brings together years of experience across compliance consulting, enterprise software development, and information security. We have worked with organizations navigating frameworks like ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, and India's DPDP Act, and we built Probity to solve the real-world problems we encountered firsthand. Our engineering team is focused on delivering a clean, reliable, and performant product that scales with your organization.

Why We Built Probity

Most GRC tools on the market are either too complex for small teams or too simplistic to handle real compliance requirements. Spreadsheets break down as organizations grow, and legacy platforms require months of implementation and training. We built Probity to fill that gap: a purpose-built platform that is powerful enough for serious compliance work yet intuitive enough to get started in minutes. From policy management and risk registers to audit tracking and framework mapping, Probity gives growing organizations the tools they need to stay compliant with confidence.
