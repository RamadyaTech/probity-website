Sub-processors

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Overview

ProbityGRC engages certain third-party service providers (sub-processors) to assist in delivering the Probity platform. Each sub-processor has been evaluated for its security practices and data-handling capabilities, and is bound by contractual obligations to protect the confidentiality and security of any data they process on our behalf.

The table below lists our current sub-processors, their purpose, and the locations where data may be processed.

Current Sub-processors

Sub-processor Name Purpose Location
DigitalOcean, LLC Cloud infrastructure hosting, compute, and managed database services. Singapore / United States
Brevo (Sendinblue) Transactional email delivery (account verification, password resets, notifications). European Union
Cloudflare, Inc. Content delivery network (CDN), DNS management, and DDoS protection. Global
Sentry (Functional Software, Inc.) Application error monitoring and performance tracking. United States
Google Analytics (Google LLC) Website analytics to measure visitor traffic and usage patterns. United States

Notification of Changes

We are committed to transparency regarding the third-party services we use. When we add, remove, or replace a sub-processor, we will:

  • Update this page with the revised sub-processor list at least 30 days before the change takes effect.
  • Notify customers with active subscriptions via email to the account owner's registered email address.
  • Provide a description of the new sub-processor's role and the location(s) where data will be processed.

If you have concerns about a newly announced sub-processor, you may raise an objection by contacting us within the 30-day notice period. We will work with you in good faith to address your concerns, which may include offering an alternative configuration where technically feasible.
