Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how ProbityGRC ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website and use the Probity platform.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function correctly, improve performance, and provide information to site operators. Cookies may be set by the website you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third-party services that appear on the page ("third-party cookies").

Cookies can be "session" cookies, which are deleted when you close your browser, or "persistent" cookies, which remain on your device for a defined period or until you delete them manually.

2. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are strictly necessary for the operation of the Probity platform. Without these cookies, core functionality such as logging in and navigating secure areas would not be possible. These cookies do not require your consent.

Cookie Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your authenticated session while you use the platform. Session
csrf_token Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks by validating form submissions. Session
auth_token Stores a secure authentication token to keep you logged in across requests. 14 days
cookie_consent Records your cookie consent preferences. 1 year

3. Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us measure and improve the performance of our site. The information collected is aggregated and anonymous.

Cookie Purpose Duration
_ga / _ga_* Google Analytics cookies used to distinguish users and track page views, session duration, and traffic sources. Up to 2 years

You may opt out of analytics tracking at any time by adjusting your cookie preferences or by using browser-level controls.

4. Preference Cookies

Preference cookies allow the platform to remember choices you have made, such as your preferred language, display settings, or dashboard layout. These cookies enhance your experience but are not strictly necessary for the platform to function.

Cookie Purpose Duration
user_preferences Stores your display and interface preferences (e.g., theme, language). 1 year
sidebar_state Remembers the collapsed or expanded state of the navigation sidebar. Session

5. How to Manage Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find these controls in the "Settings", "Preferences", or "Privacy" section of your browser. Common options include:

  • Blocking all cookies or only third-party cookies.
  • Deleting cookies that have already been set.
  • Configuring your browser to notify you before a cookie is placed.

Please note that blocking or deleting essential cookies may prevent you from logging in or using key features of the Probity platform.

For more information on managing cookies in your specific browser, please refer to your browser's help documentation:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies
